About Us

ChocoLety was founded with blood, sweat, and desperation.
As a young teen Analetizia Uliber Simono
played the legendary Chocolate Confectionary game Chocolatier 2,
and during her endevours.
She found her love for the profession and ideally named her business, ChocoLety.

The company was founded in 2019, providing confectionaries ranging from infusions,
to exotic delicacies.

ChocoLety's goal is to provide quality chocolates
with expressive quality ingredients obtained
from the far Congo, to the close supermarket sugar.
